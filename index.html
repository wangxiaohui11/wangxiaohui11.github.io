XIAOHUI WANG

wangxiaohui11@hotmail.com

 

Xiaohui Wang received her Ph.D. from the Mathematical Physics group of the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Mathematics and Computer Science, Delft University of Technology in January 2022. Her research focuses on the ocean and Atmospheric modelling, parameter estimation and observation analysis and processing.
